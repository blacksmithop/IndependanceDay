Palakkad

Palakkad also called as Palghat is the gateway to Kerala at the foot of the western ghats.
Palakkad is a land of green paddy fields and graceful tall coconut trees.

Malampuzha

Malampuzha, ‘The Vrindavan of Kerala’ is located close to Bharathapuzha at a distance of 13 km from Palakkad town. Malampuzha is a place blessed with both natural beauty and man made architecture.

Palakkad Fort

This fort situated at the heart of the city welcomes all the visitors to this marvellous place. This fort is a historically important one which tells us about the tales of many heroes of the past.
