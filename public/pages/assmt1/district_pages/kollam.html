Kollam

Kollam otherwise called as the Cashew Town is situated in the southwest corner of India in Kerala.
Kollam has an eventful past. Kollam was an international emporium of trade.

Sasthamcotta Lake

Sasthamcotta is a beautiful village situated about 19 km from Kollam Town. It is a centre of attraction and one of the most visited places in Kollam. It is a wonderful spot and pilgrimage centre and also has a health resort in here.

Thangassery

Thangassery is a historically important place situated 5 km away from Kollam town. The main attraction of this place is the lighthouse.
