Kozhikode

Kozhikode, Harbour of Honesty was once the capital of powerful Zamorins and an important trading centre in the Malabar region.
In the present days lush green countryside, serene beaches, rivers, hills, wildlife sanctuaries, unique culture
and a warm friendly atmosphere made it a favorite destination among tourists.

Mananchira Square

Mananchira Square, a man made fresh water lake situated at the heart of the city is the landmark of Kozhikode. This was originally the private bath of Zamorins. Mananchira Square has a splendid and a traditional architectural design, an open ground that is carpeted with green grass, a musical fountain and old Kerala style buildings around.

Sweet Meat Street

Sweet Meat Street popularly known as SM street starting from the Mananchira Square is the busiest shopping centre in Kozhikode. This street got its name due to the presence of numerous sweet meat stalls.
