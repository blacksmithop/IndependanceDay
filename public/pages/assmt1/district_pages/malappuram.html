Malappuram

Malappuram district in Kerala located between Kozhikode and Palakkad districts is a popular tourist attraction
with its stunning natural beauty and glorious past. Malappuram bounded by Nilgiri hills
in the east, Arabian sea in the West is a treat for the eyes.

Manjeri

Manjeri the headquarters of Eranad taluk is situated 12 Km northeast Malappuram town. Once it was a live center of the National movement. Manjeri Kovilakam was the seat of the ruling families under the Zamorins.

Pookkottur

Pookkottur near Malappuram, has a place in history, where the mappila warrior fought the British during the Malabar rebellion. Panakkad, 6 Km from malappuram is an important spiritual center for the Muslims of Kerala.
